About The Hackathon

ML4Eathon 2026 is a 48-hour Machine Learning hackathon organized by the "ML4E Club (Machine Learning for Everyone)". Participants will design and build AI-driven solutions to solve real-world challenges in healthcare, education, climate, and automation.

Why Participate?

  • Learn ML & AI in practice
  • Build projects for your portfolio
  • Collaborate with innovators
  • Win exciting prizes
